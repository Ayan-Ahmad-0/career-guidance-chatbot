# Career Guidance Chatbot

An AI-powered career guidance chatbot built with **Botpress**, designed to help users navigate career decisions, explore job paths, and get actionable advice — all through a simple conversational interface.

---

##  About

This chatbot acts as a virtual career advisor. Whether you're a fresh graduate unsure of your next step, a professional looking to switch fields, or someone exploring new opportunities, the bot provides personalized guidance through natural conversation.

It can help with:

- Exploring career paths based on your background and interests
- Understanding what skills are needed for specific roles
- Getting advice on resume building and interview preparation
- Answering questions about different industries and job markets
- Suggesting next steps based on your goals

---

##  How It Works

The chatbot is built on **Botpress**, a conversational AI platform. It uses a flow-based dialogue system combined with AI to understand user intent and respond with relevant career guidance.

Users simply type their questions or describe their situation, and the bot responds with tailored advice and suggestions.
